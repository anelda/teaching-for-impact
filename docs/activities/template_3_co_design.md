## Instructions

Decide who has a voice in designing this training.

## If you already have a training (recommended)

### Current situation

- Who designed the training?
- Who made key decisions?

---

### Power analysis

| Decision | Who currently decides? | Who should be involved? |
|----------|----------------------|--------------------------|

---

### Gaps

- Where are learners or communities excluded from decision-making?

---

### Plan for co-design

- What decisions can be shared?
- How will you involve participants meaningfully?
- What methods will you use? (workshops, feedback loops, co-creation sessions)

---

## If you are creating a new training

Define your co-design approach from the start.

---

## Reflection
- What will change if you genuinely share control?
