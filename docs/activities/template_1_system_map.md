## Instructions

Map your training as part of a real-world system.

## If you already have a training (recommended)

### Your current training
- What is the training called?
- What does it aim to do?

### System mapping

List key elements:

**Actors**
- Who is involved? (learners, trainers, institutions, funders, communities)

**Inputs**
- What resources are used? (time, tools, data, funding)

**Activities**
- What actually happens in the training?

**Outputs**
- What is produced immediately?

**Outcomes**
- What changes for participants?

**Wider system influences**
- What else affects outcomes? (context, policy, infrastructure, culture)

---

### Reality check
- What assumptions are you making about this system?
- Where are the biggest risks or uncertainties?

---

## If you are creating a new training

Map the system you are designing for using the same structure above.
